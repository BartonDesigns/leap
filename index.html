Our Approach

Built on values that
put children first.

We are committed to neurodiversity-affirming principles. These values guide every conversation, every session, and the plan we will build together.

🌱

Let Kids Lead

Children are the experts on their own experience. We follow their lead, honor their pace, and build from their strengths.

🎯

Progress Over Perfection

Growth isn't linear. We celebrate every step forward and redefine what "success" looks like for each unique child.

🎉

Celebrate Small Wins

The moments that matter most are often the quietest. A new word, a shared smile, a brave attempt. We see it all.

🤝

Model Without Expectation

We show up with patience and consistency, creating space for children to engage on their own terms and timeline.

💬

"Noncompliance" Is Communication

When a child says no, with words or behavior, they're telling us something important. We listen, not override.

🧩

All Play Is Okay

Play is how children learn, connect, and heal. There's no wrong way to play, and every form of play has value.

About the Founder

A leader in the field, now leading her own.

With over a decade of experience in Applied Behavior Analysis, the founder of LEAP has supported dozens of children and their families across a wide range of developmental needs. She brings strong clinical and operational experience, including developing training programs and mentorship for the next generation of clinicians. She remains dedicated to supporting families with individualized, evidence-based care that promotes meaningful progress.

As a mom of three, she doesn't just understand child development and behavioral challenges from a clinical perspective. She lives it every single day: the meltdowns at bedtime, the victories at the dinner table, the chaos and the joy of raising little humans. She brings both her clinical and personal experiences to offer families effective, and realistic, interventions.

For the founder, LEAP is a passion project built on the belief that every child already has what they need to thrive.
